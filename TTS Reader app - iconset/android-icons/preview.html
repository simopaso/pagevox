TTS Reader · App icon pack · Indigo

Release-ready icon set, drop-in for Android.

Adaptive icon vector drawables for Android 8.0+, density-bucketed PNG fallbacks for older devices, a themed monochrome layer for Android 13+, and a 512 × 512 hero for the Play Store. Everything is wired into the standard res/ structure — copy the folder into app/src/main/ and you're done.
